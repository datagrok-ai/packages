# Phylocanvas.gl

[_Phylocanvas.gl_](https://www.phylocanvas.gl/) is a WebLG based viewer enhanced to scale to hundreds of thousands
of leaves.

## Tree Types

_Phylocanvas.gl_ supports a variety of tree types:

* Radial
* Rectangular
* Polar
* Diagonal
* Orthogonal
